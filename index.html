About Me

Serving the public is my ultimate goal, with global and international affairs as defining features of my interests. This is exemplified in my academics and my personal pursuits. I've been inducted into the most prestigious academic societies for demonstrated excellence in both multidisciplinary studies and social sciences respectively. Although I will soon be bolstering my research experience during the fall semester, I am hoping to do the same with professional pursuits such as interning in a law firm to get more acquainted with practical legal experience. Outside of academics and professional development, I get involved with the community through my Asian-interest organization the Kappa Phi Lambda Sorority, Inc., hosting campus events pertaining to AAPI issues, community service, and fundraising for philanthropies such as CARE International and Stop It Now!. You can also find me performing with my dance team, Altitude's 5:Dirty Crew. Please read more about me and my (VERY briefly explained) experiences below and contact me if you have any questions about them or anything at all!
